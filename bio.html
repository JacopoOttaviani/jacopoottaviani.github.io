Who is Jacopo?

Jacopo Ottaviani is a computer scientist and data journalist, currently Senior Strategist at Code for Africa.

As an ICFJ Knight Fellow (2016–2019), he built teams and programmes backed by the World Bank, Google News Initiative, GIZ and the Gates Foundation.

His work blends code and storytelling, with projects published by The Guardian, Der Spiegel, El País and Internazionale, and supported by the Pulitzer Center and the European Journalism Centre.

He serves on the European Press Prize preparatory committee and the board of advisors of InfoNile, and trains journalists with BBC Media Action.
